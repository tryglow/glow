To add a Venmo link to your Glow page:

1. Login to your Glow account.

2. On the Right, click on "Blocks"

3. Drag and drop the "Link Box" or "Link Bar" block to your page.

4. Hover over the block, and click on the edit icon.

5. Add your Venmo link to the "Link" field.

Your Venmo link will now be visible on your Glow page.
