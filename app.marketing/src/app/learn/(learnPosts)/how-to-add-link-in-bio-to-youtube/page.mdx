To add a link in your YouTube channel bio:

1. Sign in to YouTube:

   - Use a browser or the YouTube app to log into your account.

2. Access Your Channel:

   - Click your profile picture in the top right, then select "Your Channel."

3. Edit Your About Section:

   - Go to the "About" tab.
   - Click on the pencil icon to edit your channel description.
   - In the description, paste the link you want to add (e.g., a website or social media profile).

4. Save Changes:

- Click "Save" to update your bio.
