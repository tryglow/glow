To add a link to your Instagram bio:

1. Open Instagram and Go to Your Profile:

   - Tap your profile icon at the bottom right corner of the app.

2. Edit Your Profile:

   - Tap the "Edit Profile" button.

3. Add Your Link:

   - Find the "Website" field and type or paste the link you want to add.
   - Tap "Done" or "Save" to update your profile.

Your link will now be visible in your bio, making it easy for your followers to click and visit the site.
